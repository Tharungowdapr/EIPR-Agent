# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Students and educators working with the EIPR (Entrepreneurship & IP Rights) curriculum. They use the platform to analyze business ideas through a structured multi-agent AI pipeline, producing comprehensive case studies aligned with the 5 EIPR units. Their primary context is academic — coursework, assignments, and curriculum exploration — not commercial decision-making.

## Product Purpose

EIPR-Agent transforms a raw business idea or domain description into a complete, structured EIPR case study by running it through 5 specialized AI agents: Opportunity Scout, IP Strategist, Business Architect, Financial Analyst, and Pedagogical Synthesizer. Success looks like a student being able to input any business concept and receive a rigorous, curriculum-aligned analysis covering opportunity discovery, IP strategy, business planning, financial feasibility, and academic synthesis — ready for review, iteration, and export.

## Brand Personality

Precise · Intelligent · Confident

Voice is authoritative but not academic-dense. Clear, direct, jargon-light. The tone conveys expertise without arrogance — like a confident tutor who makes complex topics accessible.

## Anti-references

- **Generic AI tool aesthetics**: No chatbot bubbles, robot icons, sparkle motifs, or "AI magic" messaging. This is an analytic engine, not a chat interface.
- **Gamified or playful UI**: No badges, achievement-style progress indicators, or game-like elements. The subject matter is academic and deserves a serious presentation.
- **Opaque "black box" patterns**: The AI pipeline should be transparent — show what each agent does, what stage is running, and what was produced. No mystery.

## Design Principles

1. **Content-forward, not UI-forward** — The analysis output (opportunities, IP analysis, business plans, financials, reports) is the star. UI recedes. Generous whitespace, restrained chrome, typographic hierarchy that makes complex documents readable.

2. **Academic rigor, approachable presentation** — Structured like a journal or case study (headings, sections, data callouts) but with clean modern typography and thoughtful spacing. Credible, not stuffy.

3. **Confidence through precision** — Every element has a reason. No decorative flourishes, no gradient text, no glassmorphism. Borders, spacing, and color carry meaning.

4. **Progressive disclosure of complexity** — The 5-agent pipeline is inherently complex. Each stage reveals its output clearly; the user can dive deeper or move on. The interface guides without overwhelming.

5. **Trustworthy transparency** — Show the process. When an agent runs, show loading state with what it's doing. Show the raw structured data alongside rendered views. Let users inspect, edit, and re-run any stage.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Adequate color contrast (body text ≥4.5:1 against background), keyboard-navigable interfaces, semantic HTML structure for screen readers, and reduced motion support for animations.
